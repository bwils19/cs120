Trip to Lake Como Italy

About 3 years ago I took a trip with my wife, kids and her family to Lake Como Italy. We rented a large house right on the water in Varenna. We spent a week and a half eating delicious Italian food and gelato, hiking around the lake, riding mopeds, and riding in boats around the lake. This was one of my favorite trips I have ever taken. Favorite Restaurant

My favorite restaurant is Sarma in Somerville. I have been there probably 10 times and each time I have walked away saying that that was the best meal I ever had. It is Turkish tapas and each one I've had has been delicious. They also do a dim-sum-style thing where they walk around with a tray that has differnt dishes on it that aren't on the menu. It's how they try out new recipes and I think it's genius.

My Favorite Food

I really like In-N-Out. I grew up in San Diego and was able to have it all the time.
Now I only get to eat it when I go home to visit.
There has been more than 1 occasion I have had the Uber driver drive me straight from the airport to In-N-Out before I got to see any of my friends or family.
